Conflict Studies and Insights

Conflict Studies and Insights (CS&I) is a weekly intelligence brief tracking five active conflict theatres: Russia–Ukraine, Israel–Lebanon, Israel–Gaza, Israel–US–Iran, and Thailand–Cambodia.

Each week, CS&I synthesises open-source reporting into structured operational analysis — covering key developments, tactical implications, and what they mean for planners. At the end of each month, a digest consolidates the month's insights across five warfighting functions: Manoeuvre, Sense-Strike, Combat Support, Expeditionary, and Unmanned Systems, identifying Opportunities, Vulnerabilities, and Capability Developments.

The project is powered by Claude Coworker — an AI ops learning agent that scans, analyses, and synthesises 1,000+ open-source signals weekly across multiple languages, supervised and validated by a human analyst.
